Welcome, Lash Queens!

The lashes of your dreams are only an appointment away! My name is Tiffany, a certified Lash Artist here in Louisville, KY. So come relax, take a lash nap, and listen to music in a clean, positive energy filled Lash Lab!

*PLEASE READ EVERYTHING BELOW PRIOR TO BOOKING*

Please come makeup-free and free of any oil products. If you typically wear false lashes, please make sure any false lash glue is cleaned off of your natural lashes. *There will be a cleaning fee of $10 if I need to clean any mascara or false lash residue off your natural lashes or lash extensions.*

LASH FILL-IN INFORMATION

I do not fill sets that are not mine please book removal and a full set of choice. 40% OF LASH EXTENSIONS MUST BE PRESENT IN ORDER TO BOOK A FILL-IN! Anything less than 40% of lashes will be considered a full set and you will be charged for the full set price. Please book your fill-ins in advance every 2-3 weeks. I recommend booking your fills at end of service.
